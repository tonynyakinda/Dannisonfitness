Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Do I need to be in shape to start personal training?

Not at all! Our programs are designed for all fitness levels, from complete beginners to advanced athletes. We'll start at your current level and progress from there.

What should I bring to my first session?

Just bring workout clothes, athletic shoes, a water bottle, and a towel. We provide all necessary equipment and amenities.

How soon can I expect to see results?

Most clients notice improvements in energy, mood, and strength within 2-3 weeks. Visible physical changes typically appear within 4-8 weeks with consistent training and proper nutrition.

Do you offer package discounts?

Yes, we offer discounted rates for session packages. The more sessions you purchase, the greater the discount per session. We also offer family and couple packages.
